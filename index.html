About The Awja

Awja is an expanding brand with a leading interest in premium food, beverages and eco-friendly products. Awja guarantees to have an influence and essence of nature in its products. It is an elegant resort set up by Mrs Chaitali Chakma, offering an excellent banquet with a variety of food and beverage choices. Under her leadership, the team works with perfect coordination to bring unique delicacies right onto your plates. Awja aims to be more sustainable by using eco-friendly crockery and solar panels to reduce fossil fuel use, with many more sustainable initiatives planned in the future.
